Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy (গোপনীয়তার নীতি)

Effective Date: June 09, 2026

আসহাবে রাসুলের জীবনকথা (referred to as "the App") is developed by Bendroid Apps. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a safe experience while using our application.

1 Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

When you sign in using Google Auth, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Profile Picture URL
  • User ID (UID)

This information is used to manage your account, sync your reading progress across devices, and manage your premium subscription status via Google Firebase.

B. Reading Progress

We store your reading history and last read positions of Sahabi biographies locally and on Firestore to help you continue where you left off.

C. Device Information

For app performance and advertising purposes, we may collect:

  • Device model and OS version
  • Unique device identifiers (for AdMob advertisements)
  • Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics

2 How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide access to biography content and save your progress.
  • Premium Management: To verify payments and provide ad-free access.
  • Notifications: To send reading reminders (if enabled).
  • Advertising: To show relevant ads through Google AdMob for free users.

3 Third-Party Services

The App utilizes third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Authentication & Firestore
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob (Privacy Policy)

4 Payment Verification

For premium upgrades, we use a manual bKash verification system. We only collect the Transaction ID and Mobile Number provided by you to approve your subscription. We do not have access to your bKash PIN or OTP.

5 Data Retention & Security

Your account data is stored securely in Google Firebase. You can request to delete your account and all associated data by contacting us via email.
